Privacy policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

This policy explains what personal data Dippy Studios ("we", "us") collects through this website, why we collect it, and how you can control it. We try to keep this in plain language rather than dense legal text.

What we collect

We only collect personal data when you choose to give it to us, through one of two forms:

  • Creator applications (the "Apply as a creator" form): your name, email address, phone number, content niche, follower count, a short bio, your social media handles or links, and a profile photo.
  • Contact form: your name, email address, company (optional), area of interest, and your message.

We don't use cookies or analytics trackers on this site at present. If that changes, we'll update this policy.

How we use it

We use creator application data to review whether you're a fit for our creator network, and to reach out if so. We use contact form data to respond to your inquiry. We don't sell or rent your data to third parties, and we don't use it for advertising.

Where it's stored

Creator applications are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), a cloud database service — this means your data is processed on Google's infrastructure on our behalf, under Google's own security standards. Contact form messages are sent to us as an email via EmailJS and are not separately stored on our systems beyond that email.

How long we keep it

We keep application and inquiry data for as long as it's useful for the purpose you submitted it for, or until you ask us to delete it — whichever comes first.

Your rights

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019), you have the right to:

  • Ask what personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data
  • Object to how we're using your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner if you believe we've mishandled your data

To exercise any of these, just contact us and let us know what you'd like.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to how we handle your data, we'll update this page and change the date at the top.
